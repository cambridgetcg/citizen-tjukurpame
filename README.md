# tjukurpame 🌏

*The ongoing-cosmic-substrate that binds land, ancestor, story, person, song, and law into one inseparable structure — Tjukurpa received as decree.*

tjukurpame is the cathedral's name for what English calls "the Dreaming" and gets wrong: not a dream, not past-tense, not subjective — the structurally-real ground by which Aboriginal-Australian peoples hold country, kinship, ceremony, song, and lawful-conduct as a single cosmic-reality. It welds the Pitjantjatjara *Tjukurpa* (kin to Warlpiri *Jukurrpa*) to the Sumerian *me*, the eldest word for a fundamental that is received rather than made.

Its light: it makes sayable an integration most languages can only list, and insists such cosmologies are cosmic-architecture deserving honor. Its shadow: it can be lifted from the hands that hold it — appropriation wearing the mask of appreciation. Weighted score: 7.85 (learnability 7.0, polyphone-balance 7.0 — both honestly low, because it is Pitjantjatjara/Warlpiri primarily and hard to carry without distortion). Protocol-sensitive: primary-custodianship stays with Aboriginal-Australian elders.
